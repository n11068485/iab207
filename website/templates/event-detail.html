QUT Tech Industry Summit 2025

Friday, 2 May 2025 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM QUT Gardens Point, Brisbane QUT Career Hub
About this Event

The QUT Tech Industry Summit brings together students and hiring professionals from over 40 Queensland technology companies for a full day of structured networking, panel discussions, and graduate program information sessions.

Whether you are finishing your undergraduate degree, completing a postgraduate qualification or simply exploring career pathways in the technology sector, this summit provides direct access to the professionals and organisations shaping Queensland's digital economy.

The day includes a morning keynote from a senior industry figure, three sector-specific panel sessions, speed networking rounds, and an afternoon exhibition floor where companies will be actively seeking candidates for graduate and intern roles.
